About Us

The company that brought you Nu.Trac, we present to you PawPii! PawPii is a resource dedicated to assisting Adoption Shelters connect with caring individuals to add a four-legged friend to their family. With puppies at the root of our brand, we believe that families should experience the love and trust they can bring.
